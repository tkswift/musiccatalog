<h1>Music Catalog</h1>

<p>Music Catalog is an iOS app for browsing, searching, and managing your music collection. Scan barcodes, search releases, organize folders, manage your wantlist, and edit your profile — all from your iPhone or iPad.</p>

- **Track your music catalog** - View your entire music collection in a single place
- **Scan barcodes** — Quickly add releases to your collection by scanning barcodes using your devices camera
- **Manage your wantlist** — Add, edit notes, and remove wantlist items
- **Organize your music** — Organize your collection into folders
- **View your profile and collection value** — Update your Discogs profile and see your estimated collection value

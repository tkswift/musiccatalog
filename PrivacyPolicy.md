# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 4, 2026

Music Catalog uses the Discogs API to retrieve data. Music Catalog does **not** collect or store your data.

---

## Ads and analytics

Music Catalog does not display ads or provide information to ad providers.

## Information usage

Music Catalog uses the Discogs API to retrieve your collection, wantlist, and profile related data. This data is never shared.

## Data Collected by the Developer

**None.** Music Catalog has no servers or databases.

## Data Transmitted to Third Parties

### Discogs

Music Catalog is a client for the [Discogs](https://www.discogs.com) API.  

[Discogs Privacy Policy](https://www.discogs.com/privacy)

## Camera Access

Camera access is requested solely for **barcode scanning** to find releases. No images or video are ever captured, recorded, stored, or transmitted. You can deny camera permission and still use all other features via text search.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For questions, please contact [music.catalog.manager@proton.me](mailto:music.catalog.manager@proton.me).
